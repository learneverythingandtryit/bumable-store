Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with Bumable Clothing

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our collection, select your desired items, choose size and quantity, add to cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your shipping information and complete the payment process.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. Contact our customer service immediately. Once the order is processed and shipped, modifications are not possible.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and popular digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, while express shipping takes 2-3 business days. Free shipping is available on orders above â‚¹999 and takes 5-7 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our shipping to international locations soon.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on the courier company's website.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return window from delivery date. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with original tags and packaging. Contact us to initiate a return.

How long does it take to process refunds?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item. The amount will be credited back to your original payment method.

Can I exchange an item for a different size?

Yes, exchanges are available for size or color variations within 7 days, subject to stock availability. Contact our customer service to arrange an exchange.

How do I choose the right size?

Each product page has a detailed size guide. Measure yourself and compare with our size chart. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a comfortable fit.

Are the colors accurate to what I see online?

We strive for color accuracy, but slight variations may occur due to screen settings and lighting. We photograph our products in natural lighting to show true colors.

What materials do you use for your clothing?

We use high-quality materials including cotton, cotton blends, and sustainable fabrics. Each product page lists the exact material composition and care instructions.

How do I contact customer service?

You can contact us through the contact form on our website, email us directly, or reach out via our social media channels. We respond within 24 hours.

Do you have a physical store?

Currently, we are an online-only store. This allows us to offer competitive prices and a wide selection while maintaining quality and fast shipping.

Didn't find what you're looking for?

Our customer support team is here to help you with any questions not covered in our FAQ.
