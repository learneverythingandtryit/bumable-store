Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about our shipping and return policies

Shipping Information

Shipping Methods & Timeline

Standard Shipping

₹50 - Delivered within 5-7 business days

Express Shipping

₹100 - Delivered within 2-3 business days

Free Shipping

FREE - On orders above ₹999 (5-7 business days)

Shipping Coverage

  • We ship across India to most PIN codes
  • Some remote locations may have extended delivery times
  • Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location
  • Orders are processed Monday through Friday (excluding holidays)

Return Policy

Return Window

We offer a 7-day return window from the date of delivery. Items must be returned in their original condition with tags attached.

Eligible Items

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • All original tags and packaging must be included
  • Items should not have any stains, odors, or damage
  • Certain items like undergarments may not be eligible for return due to hygiene reasons

Return Process

  1. Contact Us: Reach out through our contact form or customer service
  2. Return Authorization: We'll provide you with return instructions and shipping label
  3. Package Items: Securely pack items with original packaging and tags
  4. Ship Back: Use the provided shipping label and drop off at the courier
  5. Processing: Refund will be processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the return

Exchange Policy

  • Exchanges are available for size or color variations within 7 days
  • Subject to stock availability
  • Exchange shipping charges may apply
  • Contact customer service to initiate an exchange

Refund Information

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method
  • Processing time: 5-7 business days for credit/debit cards
  • Processing time: 3-5 business days for digital wallets
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive damaged or defective items:

  • Contact us immediately with photos of the damaged item
  • We'll arrange for immediate replacement or full refund
  • No need to return damaged items unless specifically requested
  • We'll cover all shipping costs for defective item replacements

Need Help?

For any shipping or return queries, please contact our customer support team. We're here to help make your shopping experience smooth and hassle-free!
